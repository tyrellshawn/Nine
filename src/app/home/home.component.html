The Problem:
In Nigeria there has been a major leap forward in recent years in terms of mobile Connectivity. This has lead to a rise in Social Media and Networking popularity.
However in the Era of Crypto and BlockChain Tech there has been demand for a new type of Payment Platform. A mobile System that Allows Users to Transfer and Lend Money Seamlessy. In addition this platform would also work as a checkout option for individuals and groups.
This Platform is Nine and its Three core functionalities would provide features that would help Usher in a New World of Oppurtunities for Businesses and Young Entrepeneurs.
Our Solution: This Platform is Nine and its Three core functionalities would provide features that would help Usher in a New World of Oppurtunities for Businesses and Young Entrepeneurs.
The Application
Transfers
With Nine Transfer, Users can make instant payments to local Users in a Nine Supported Region. Users will be able to get instant transactions at low rates with encrypted technology to prevent third party interference.
IOU Option
The Nine.IOU is the Next Generation way of Lending Money. “The goal is to provide a new credit system that incentivezes lenders and promotes borrowing”. Users create there own conditions that meet our minimized guidelines. The goal is then to promote a Mobilized Open and Engaged Market in Western Africa and around the World.
Gateway Integration
Nine Gateway is reliant on an Association with our Supported Platforms but this would allow checkout options on your favorite sites with our Gateway Home. Our Targeted platforms would be Amazon, Shopify, and Googles Cloud Platform.
